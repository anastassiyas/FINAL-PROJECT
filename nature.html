    • Trans-Ili Alatau

  • Trans-Ili Alatau. One of the most beautiful places in Kazakhstan. Beautiful mountain landscapes, glaciers, mountain rivers, picturesque valleys.

    • Charyn canyon

  • Charyn canyon is a deep gorge in Kazakhstan, in the valley of the Charyn river, near the border with China and Kyrgyzstan. Given the size and geological features of the Charyn canyon, it is often referred to as the" younger brother " of the American Grand Canyon.
