Traditional food

  • The main traditional Kazakh dishes are meat.
  • Lamb, beef and horse meat are used for cooking. Traditional meat dishes: beshparmak, kuyrdak, manty, plov, kazy-karta.
  • In addition to meat dishes, there is a wide variety of dairy dishes and drinks: koumiss (fermented milk), shubat (sour camel milk),
    ayran (a type of kefir), kaymak (sour cream), kilegey (cream), Sary-may (salted butter), Kurt (hard salted cottage cheese) and others
  • Traditional bread of three types: baursaks — round or square pieces of dough fried in boiling oil in a cauldron,tandoor tortillas —
    baked on the inside of a tandoor oven and shelpek-tortillas that are fried in boiling oil.
